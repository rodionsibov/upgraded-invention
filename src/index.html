In conclusion, any savvy car owner is well informed about the importance of car maintenance and repair procedures.

The auto service process helps you to ensure that all the critical aspects of your vehicle are in optimal performance.

How it is Done?

Broadly speaking, there are various types of cars, with as many different components. As a result, they often have as different auto service requirements. Besides that, there are multiple types of auto services available on the consumer market today.

An auto service is a more encompassing car care approach, which might involve addressing various aspects of the vehicle. Some of the components of the car which are addressed include:

Brake System and Oil Change

This includes the evaluation of the brake pedal, the hydraulic brake fluid, power booster, sensors and more. More so, oil is an essential aspect of the engine, whereby it provides lubrication and reduces wear.

Without the cleaning effects of fresh oil, carbon and varnish can accumulate, thereby compromising the engine.

Tire Repair

Tires are also essential and should be inspected through various techniques. For instance, the service provider will evaluate the tires through rotation and special balancing techniques.

If the treads are worn out, then it might be the right time to get the tires replaced.

Exhaust System Services

Most people think the exhaust system only reduces noise from the engine. However, it is also responsible for the controlling the harmful emissions of gases into the atmosphere.

The ideal exhaust system consists of mufflers and special filters, which require adequate maintenance.

Check Engine Light

When you car experiences the famous "check engine light", this means that it is time for you to evaluate the engine performance of your vehicle as soon as possible.

A professional mechanic will help you evaluate the engine light codes, and address any repair issues on time. Note that any delays in addressing the engine light might lead to secondary car engine complications.

Belts and Hoses

Sooner or later, the various other components of your car will require maintenance and servicing. Some of these accessories include the drive belts, coolant hoses, timing belts, just to name a few.

Performing consistent evaluations along with timely automotive hose and belt replacement will help in reducing the effects of roadside breakdowns and costly repairs.

What do you mean by "Figma assets"?

You will have access to download the full Figma project including all of the pages, the components, responsive pages, and also the icons, illustrations, and images included in the screens.

What does "lifetime access" exactly mean?

Once you have purchased either the design, code, or both packages, you will have access to all of the future updates based on the roadmap, free of charge.

How does support work?

We're aware of the importance of well qualified support, that is why we decided that support will only be provided by the authors that actually worked on this project.

Feel free to contact us and we'll help you out as soon as we can.

I want to build more than one project. Is that allowed?

You can use Windster for an unlimited amount of projects, whether it's a personal website, a SaaS app, or a website for a client. As long as you don't build a product that will directly compete with Windster either as a UI kit, theme, or template, it's fine.

Find out more information by reading the license.

What does "free updates" include?

The free updates that will be provided is based on the roadmap that we have laid out for this project. It is also possible that we will provide extra updates outside of the roadmap as well.

What does the free version include?

The free version of Windster includes a minimal style guidelines, component variants, and a dashboard page with the mobile version alongside it.

You can use this version for any purposes, because it is open-source under the MIT license.

What is the difference between Windster and Tailwind UI?

Although both Windster and Tailwind UI are built for integration with Tailwind CSS, the main difference is in the design, the pages, the extra components and UI elements that Windster includes.

Additionally, Windster is a project that is still in development, and later it will include both the application, marketing, and e-commerce UI interfaces.

Can I use Windster in open-source projects?

Generally, it is accepted to use Windster in open-source projects, as long as it is not a UI library, a theme, a template, a page-builder that would be considered as an alternative to Windster itself.

With that being said, feel free to use this design kit for your open-source projects.
